  • New customers starting a recurring contract require an initial deep clean visit at €75/hr (min. 3h).
  • All professional cleaning supplies, eco-certified products, and equipment included — no surprise fees.
  • Steam cleaning is included at no extra cost in all deep clean, move-out, and post-renovation services.
  • Extraction cleaning drying time: 4–8 hours. We recommend booking before a day off.
  • Cancellation: free with 48 hours notice. Less than 48 hours: 50% charge applies.
  • We operate across Tampere, Kangasala, Ylöjärvi, Lempäälä, Pirkkala, and Nokia.
  • Travel outside 20km radius of Tampere centre: surcharge from €30.
  • Detailed annual invoices provided for tax deduction claims at no extra cost.
  • Quality guarantee: if unsatisfied, we return and re-clean the area free of charge within 48 hours.
